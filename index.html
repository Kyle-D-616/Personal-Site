About Me

Hello! I’m Kyle Dukes, a dedicated Software Developer and Data Engineer with a deep passion for technology and learning.

With over 17 years of diverse professional experience, including 5 years in managerial roles across various fields, I’ve developed a broad skill set that I now apply to software and data engineering. Although my career path seemed eclectic at times, it ultimately led me to my true calling: coding. The varied experiences I've had uniquely position me for success in this field.

I am entirely self-taught, with formal education limited to general studies. My career journey has honed essential skills such as time management, workflow optimization, research, and problem-solving. Over the past three years, I’ve mastered the art of learning—understanding both how and what to learn has become a key strength of mine. The challenges I’ve faced have fueled my resilience and deepened my passion for technology. Thanks to valuable mentorship and experiences, I’ve amassed a wealth of knowledge and skills.

For the past three years, I’ve been immersed in development, with over two years in a development role at a smaller company. This environment has allowed me to wear multiple hats: primarily as a data engineer, but also as a data scientist and software engineer. I work extensively with SQL in Microsoft SQL Management Studio for data extraction, loading, and transformation. I am proficient in various programming languages, with a focus on Python, C#, HTML, and CSS. My role also involves gathering customer requirements and presenting data in an accessible manner.
